What is Customer Lifetime Value?

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) estimates the total revenue a customer or segment will generate for your business over their relationship with you. Instead of optimizing for just the next order, CLV encourages long-term, relationship-focused decisions.

DASTATS CLV combines transaction history, behavior, engagement, and churn patterns to predict how valuable a customer is likely to be‚Äîand what actions you can take to grow that value.

  • Design acquisition campaigns around high-CLV profiles.
  • Prioritize retention, upsell, and win-back programs where it matters.
  • Align marketing, sales, and product around long-term customer value.
