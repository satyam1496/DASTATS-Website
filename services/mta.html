What is Multi-Touch Attribution?

Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA) assigns value to every meaningful touchpoint in a customer‚Äôs journey‚Äîimpressions, clicks, visits, and engagements‚Äîrather than crediting only the first or last interaction.

Why it matters

  • Understand which channels assist vs close conversions.
  • Stop over-crediting branded search or retargeting alone.

  • Confidently re-allocate budgets based on full-funnel impact.
